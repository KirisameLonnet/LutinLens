Libre Camera es un application de camera libere e de codice aperte pro prender photos e registrar videos scribite in Flutter e Dart.

Si tu besonia un application de camera de codice aperte con alcun functionalitates, iste app pote esser pro te. De luce flash, controlos de foco e exposition a cambios de thema, linguas e quantitate de compression – iste app deberea haber lo. E si non, tu pote aperir un issue sur <a href="https://github.com/iakmds/librecamera">GitHub</a> e demandar un characteristica. Infra tu pote videl un lista de functionalitates, con multe plus a venir in le futur!

<b>Functionalitates</b>
    * Prender imagines e registrar videos con le cameras frontal e posterior
    * Confidentialitate: Nulle metadatos EXIF salveguardate per predefinition al prender imagines (enable optionally in settings) – sin publicitate, traceamento o permission es non necessari
    * Themas: Material Design con themas clar, obscur o de systema
    * Multiple linguas supportate– Contribue in le traduction de tu lingua!
    * Zoom: Zoom con duo digitos o un glissator optional
    * Timer/Delay: Select a photo capture timer to get ready for your photo
    * Flashlight modes: On at capture, Off, Auto, Always on
    * Focus: Auto focus, locked focus and manual focus by touching the screen
    * Exposure: Auto exposure, locked exposure and manual exposure with an optional slider
    * Image compression: Set the compression amount in the settings
    * Formats: Switch between 3 camera formats (JPEG, PNG, WebP)
    * Resolutions: Switch between different camera resolutions
    * Save location: Choose your preferred save location
    * Small size of about 21MB
    * Customizable: Open the settings to customize the app to your liking!
